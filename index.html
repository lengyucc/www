How it works?

01

Register with us

After following all the steps of product registration, your profile will be manually verified. Once it is done, your product will become available to all the sales consultant on our platform to sell.

02

Independent sales consultants learn about your product

Thoroughly vetted independent sales consultant learns about your product and starts reaching out to companies in their network and geography whose problem your product solves. Our AI prospecting tool comes handy for better prospecting

03

Lead nurturing and deal closing

Independent sales consultants educate the buyer on the problem which your product is solving and deploys the product with the help of Flinkhub's collaborative AI and tools.

04

Deal completed

Once the product is sold, you pay the commission on a monthly or annual basis depending on the deal and our sales consultants stay available to any need of the buyer keeping your customers happy.
